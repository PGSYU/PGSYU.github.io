Membership Benefits

  • Support for New Graduate Students: Assistance in navigating campus life, academic processes, and settling into York University.
  • Platform for a Support System: A community-driven platform to share experiences, provide mentorship, and offer guidance for academic and personal growth.
  • Access to a Supportive Network: Connect with fellow Pakistani graduate students for collaboration, cultural exchange, and building lasting relationships.
  • Regular Events and Workshops: Participate in activities for social gatherings.
  • Advocacy and Representation: Voice your concerns and contribute to initiatives that address the needs of Pakistani graduate students at York University.

Mission & Goals

Mission

To support Pakistani graduate students at York University by creating a welcoming community that understands their unique needs. We connect students for academic support, professional growth, and cultural activities while helping them navigate life as graduate students in Canada.

Goals

  • Help new Pakistani graduate students settle into university life and Toronto.
  • Create study groups and academic support networks within different graduate programs.
  • Get to know other graduate students in various academic programs.
  • Connect students with successful York grad alumni working in their field for career guidance.
  • Organize social events that fit graduate students' schedules and interests (like dinner gatherings, coffee meetups, and family-friendly activities).
  • Help members with practical challenges like finding housing, understanding immigration processes, and job hunting.
  • Work with other university Pakistani faculty members to build stronger networks.

Constitution

Article I: Name

The name of this organization shall be the Pakistani Graduate Students Association, hereinafter referred to as PGS.

Article II: Mission

To support Pakistani graduate students at York University by creating a welcoming community that understands their unique needs. PGS connects students for academic support, professional growth, and cultural activities while assisting them in navigating life as graduate students in Canada.

Article III: Goals

  • Orientation and Settling In: Assist new Pakistani graduate students in settling into university life and the city of Toronto.
  • Academic Support: Create study groups and academic support networks within different graduate programs.
  • Networking: Facilitate connections among graduate students across various academic disciplines. Connect students with successful York graduate alumni for career guidance.
  • Social Engagement: Organize social events that accommodate graduate students’ schedules and interests, such as dinner gatherings, coffee meetups, and family-friendly activities.
  • Practical Assistance: Help members with practical challenges, including finding housing, understanding immigration processes, and job hunting.
  • Collaboration with Faculty: Work with Pakistani faculty members at the university to build stronger academic and professional networks.

Article IV: Membership

Eligibility:

  • Membership is open to all current Pakistani graduate students enrolled at York University.
  • York graduate alumni and other Pakistanis in special cases.

Admission:

  • Prospective members must complete the official registration process and pay any applicable dues.

Rights and Responsibilities:

  • Members have the right to attend all events, access resources, and vote in general meetings.
  • Members are expected to uphold the mission and values of PGS and participate actively in its initiatives.
